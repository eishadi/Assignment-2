Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Focuses on language, basic math, creativity, social-emotional development, physical activities, and moral values.

Elementary School

Covers literacy, numeracy, science, social studies, arts, physical education, and character-building foundations.

Middle (10-16) Years

Includes advanced math, sciences, languages, history, arts, critical thinking, physical health, and life skills.
